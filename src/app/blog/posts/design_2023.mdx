# My Design In 2023

In 2023, the second year I started college—around the third or fourth semester, when you’re exploring everything the campus has to offer—I became interested in design and decided to get involved in several campus committees, join organization and participate in the UIUX competiton. Below are the names of the events and my design work for them.

### Oprec Bem 2023/2024

During my first term with the BEM Fasilkom Unej organization as a multimedia staff member, I was responsible for creating recruitment posters and GForm headers for new member registration.

![Oprec Bem 2023](/images/blog/design23/oprec_bem.png)

### Feed IG BEM Fasilkom

During my term in office, I was responsible for creating the Instagram feed for BEM Fasilkom Unej, and I also served on several committees while working as a multimedia staff member. Here are some of the designs I created for the BEM Fasilkom Unej Instagram feed.

![Feed IG BEM Fasilkom](/images/blog/design23/feed_bem.png)


### Friendship 2023

This is an inter-departmental competition at the Faculty of Computer Science, Unej, featuring a variety of sports events—ranging from traditional sports to esports—as well as several other technology-related competitions. As a member of the PDD staff, I created the feed design, Instagram Stories, and a booklet for the event.

![Friendship 2023](/images/blog/design23/fs.png)

### Cover Booklet PKKMB

As a head of the PDD staff in PKKMB 2023, I was responsible for designing the booklet cover and the booklet itself for the PKKMB event, as a request by the organizing committee.

![Friendship 2023](/images/blog/design23/cover_pkkmb.png)

### Feed PKKMB 2023

As a head of the PDD staff in PKKMB 2023, I was responsible for designing the Instagram feed for the PKKMB event,  Instagram feed management is carried out by creating designs based on a mutually agreed-upon theme.

![Feed PKKMB 2023](/images/blog/design23/pkkmbf.png)

### Oprec Lab AI

As a multimedia member of the Student Executive Board (BEM), I sometimes receive requests to design posters outside of my regular organizational duties; one such request was to create a recruitment poster for the AI lab.

![Oprec Lab AI](/images/blog/design23/lab_ai.png)
