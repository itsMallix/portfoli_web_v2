# My Design In 2022

In 2022, the year I started college—around the second or third semester, when you’re exploring everything the campus has to offer—I became interested in design and decided to get involved in several campus committees. Below are the names of the events and my design work for them.

### Laos Arena 2022

Laos Arena is a faculty-level competition in the field of cybersecurity, specifically CTF. In this event, I served as a committee member in the PDD division and designed the event poster.

![Laos Arene 2022](/images/blog/design22/laos_arena.png)

### Pelatihan Linux

This program offers an opportunity for high school students interested in learning the Linux operating system for networking, servers, and system administration. I served as a comittee memeber in the PDD division and designed the poster and banner event.

![Pelatihan Linux 2022](/images/blog/design22/pelatihan_linux.png)

### Bookmark Design Competition

I once entered a bookmark design contest on Instagram @book_off_indonesia. I created the design using CorelDRAW, based on the concept of reading a book a second time to catch messages or details I missed the first time around. Unfortunately, my design wasn’t selected as a finalist.

![Bookmark Design 2022](/images/blog/design22/bookmark.png)

### Madrasah Explore Campus 2022

An annual event held at my high school, where the organizing committee invites all alumni currently attending college at various universities to come back to the school to provide information about admissions at their respective universities. As the Design Coordinator, my role was to design the event banner.

![Madrasah Explore Campus 2022](/images/blog/design22/mec.png)

### MKWU Apprisol

For our entrepreneurship course assignment to sell a product of our choice, our group decided to focus on the food and beverage sector and chose risol as our product. As the CTO, I was responsible for designing social media posts, posters, and other marketing materials.

![MKWU Apprisol](/images/blog/design22/apprisol.png)

### Poster Competition Earth Day

The Earth Day poster contest was organized by the Balwana Outdoor Club of the Faculty of Computer Science at the University of Jember. I registered as a participant and was tasked with creating a poster design for the Earth Day webinar. Unfortunately, my design was not selected as a nominee.

![Poster Competition Earth Day 2022](/images/blog/design22/harbum.png)

### Pelatihan UIUX

A training session organized by the LAOS student club at the Faculty of Computer Science, University of Jember, to provide students with information about what UX/UI design is and how to build a career in the field. As a PDD staff member, I was tasked with designing the countdown poster for the training session.

![Pelatihan UIUX 2022](/images/blog/design22/pelatihan_uiux.png)

### Makrab BEM 2022/2023

An overnight event aimed at fostering solidarity and camaraderie among the organization’s members. In this role, I served as the Design Coordinator, responsible for designing the event banner and documenting the event from start to finish.

![Makrab BEM 2022/2023](/images/blog/design22/makrab.png)
