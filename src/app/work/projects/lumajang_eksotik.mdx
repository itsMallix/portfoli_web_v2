## Overview

Public reporting and information center for Lumajang Eksotik, a community to goverment reporting platform, .

## Key Features

- **Reporting**: Community can complaint about their facilities on their region to goverment.
- **Information Center**: Community can see all about goverment policies.
- **News**: Community can see all news around their region.
- **Tourism**: Community can see all tourism place in their region.

## Technologies Used

- **Figma**: For creating and managing design assets and prototypes.
- **Flutter**: For android mobile development.
- **Firebase**: For login authentication and cloud data storage.
- **BLoC**: For state management.

## Challenges and Learnings

Challanges was ensuring that the wroted code was clean and maintainable because the apps will be used by many people, so the code must be readable and maintainable and the app must be lightweight and fast.

## Outcome

The app is completed and available on google play store.
