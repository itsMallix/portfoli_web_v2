## Overview

UI/UX design for a community and gamification apps for PMO (porn, masturbation and orgasm) addicts.

## Key Features

- **Distraction Button**: Emergency button can be accessed from any where in the app while the user feel urges.
- **Set Goals and Relaps**: Determine the target number of days that must pass without PMO so that they can create more measurable goals.
- **Challenge**: Creating daily challenges tailored to their interests and abilities in an effort to change habits and routines for the better.
- **Community**: Interact more broadly with users who feel the same way and also function like social media in general.
- **Gamification**: Adopting principles from the gaming world to increase their engagement in achieving goals in an exciting and enjoyable way

## Technologies Used

- **Figma**: For creating and managing design assets and prototypes.

## Challenges and Learnings

One of the biggest challenges was interact with PMO addicts, beause most of them very sensitive to topic and don't want to discuss or interviewed.
